Galada is a Bengali and Latin font that started with the famous Latin Lobster font, and extended the design to Bengali. The Bengali was developed as a studio collaboration by Jeremie Hornus, Yoann Minet, and Juan Bruce. The Galada project is led by Black Foundry, a type design foundry based in Paris, France.
